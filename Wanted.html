Project Overview

Wanted: Yokai Uprising is a cooperative first-person shooter. The game was created over 10 months by a team of 16 people (7 artists, 7 programmers, and 2 designers) and was released on Steam. Sound effects and music were crafted in collaboration with Berklee College of Music.

I was resposible for the implementation of all in-game animations, for every character and weapon, including the player character and enemies. This included creating state machines, setting up blending and blend spaces, layered animations, allowing for smooth character movement in different directions and simultaneous animations for the lower and upper body. As well as implementing a fisics-based animation (IK Rig)system for the player character, allowing for realistic reactions to the environment and interactions with objects and enemies.

Key Features

Animations

Implemented all in-game animations, including state machines, blending, and event triggers.

Physics-Based Animation

Implemented a physics-based animation system for realistic character interactions with the environment.

Custom IK Rig

Created a custom inverse kinematics rig for realistic character movement and interactions.
